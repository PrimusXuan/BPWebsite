About DebateHub

DebateHub was founded by a team of debate champions and coaches who saw the need for a comprehensive platform that brings together all aspects of English debate preparation.

Our mission is to make high-quality debate resources accessible to students of all ages, from elementary school to university level, covering all major debate formats and competitions.

What makes us unique is our integrated approach—combining competition information, professional editing services, and a training & practice system in one platform.
